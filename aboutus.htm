Dennis Rowlader Builder, LLC has been providing quality, licensed residential construction in West Michigan for over 14 years. Whether you are looking to remodel part of your current home, add rooms, a garage, or a new deck, or completely start from scratch with a custom home designed around you, we are able to meet virtually any of your building needs.

Based in Hudsonville, we've completed projects everywhere from Hastings to Sparta to the Lakeshore. Dennis (Gus) believes firmly in combining the best quality materials with fair pricing and guaranteed construction on every project he does.
